Why?

Keeping a Digital Portfolio is important because it shows your growth throughout your highschool career and can serve as your main portfolio website and a resume after highschool. It represents a student's individuality and skills for academic and professional opportunities. Once a student graduates high school, they graduate with more than a transcript, leaving school with a lifetime tool. Digital Portflios can also act as a resumeand help students stand out from the competition and establish professionalism while providing evidence of accomplishments and skills. That is why Digital Portfolio is important to me.
